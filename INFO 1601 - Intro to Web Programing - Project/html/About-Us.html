About Us


Mission

Our aim is to provide an online marketplace where students can sell and purchase pre-owned items at a reasonable cost without the hassle of organizing and processing.


With this new website, no longer are students required to post in various social media groups to indicate their interest in buying or selling, nor required to perform face-to-face transaction at mutually convenient time and place for both the buyer and seller; thus, eliminating the perilous nature of meeting unfamiliar persons solely for the purpose of goods for cash.

Prior to the implimentation of this website, students could only purchase compact personal items, such as books and school material from this storefront, but thanks to the newly employed delivery system, we have extended the options a little further. Electronics, furniture and kitchen supplies can now be sold via the click of a mouse!
